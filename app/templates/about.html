About the project

Built to support clinical and scientific information exploration.

ClinAgent is designed to make the exploration of clinical trials and biomedical information faster, more accessible, and easier to navigate through a focused conversational interface.

It combines large language models, retrieval workflows, and domain-oriented data access to help users work through research questions in a more structured way.

Collaboration

This project brings together technical and domain expertise across computer engineering, modeling, systems, pharmacy, health, and nutrition sciences.

Important notice

The platform relies on generative AI. Outputs may contain inaccuracies, omissions, or misleading interpretations and should always be reviewed critically.
